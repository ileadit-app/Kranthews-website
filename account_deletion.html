Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your iLeadIt account and all associated data, please follow the steps below. This process is in accordance with our privacy policy and Google Play requirements.

  1. Email us at admin@kranthews.com from the email address associated with your iLeadIt account.
  2. In your email, please include:
    • Your full name (optional, for verification)
    • The email address linked to your iLeadIt account
    • Any additional information to help us verify your identity
  3. We will process your request within 7 days and notify you once your account and all associated data have been permanently deleted.
Data Deletion Details:
All personal data, activity history, and account information will be permanently deleted from our servers. No data is retained after deletion. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
